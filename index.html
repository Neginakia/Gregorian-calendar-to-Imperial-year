Pastel Calendar Converter

Change dates between Gregorian, Solar Hijri, and Imperial Iranian years.

Pick a source date, choose the calendar you want, and get the converted year, month, and day with a soft pastel layout in English or Persian.

How it works

The Imperial Iranian calendar uses the same Solar Hijri months and days, but the year is shown with a royal era offset. This tool lets you convert in both directions through a Python-powered date engine by Negin Kianimarvast.
